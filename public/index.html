How to play

Soldiers appear from the entry points, and head towards your base.

You can choose weapons from the menu on the left, and place them on the map if you have enough money.

You earn a bounty by killing the soldiers.

By extending the distance the soldiers have to travel, you can increase your opportunities for firing at them.

Clicking on a deployed unit gives you options to sell it or upgrade it.
